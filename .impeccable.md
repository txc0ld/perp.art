# Design Context - perpetual.art

## Product
A permanence-first NFT marketplace. OpenSea-grade trading UX, built on one guarantee:
the artwork is provably permanent and survives even if the operator disappears (parallel
immutable copies across onchain/ethfs, IPFS, Arweave, Irys, backstopped by an onchain proof).

## Target audience
- Permanence-conscious digital artists (primary supply side) burned by broken NFTs / abandoned royalties.
- Serious collectors holding meaningful value who fear NFT decay and want provable permanence.
- Institutional / archival buyers (museums, foundations, estates) needing legal-grade permanence.
- Volume traders/flippers (tertiary) who need OpenSea-grade UX and liquidity.

## Use cases
Browse/discover, search, view collections and token detail, verify per-shard permanence,
buy (gasless, fee + royalty shown pre-confirmation), make offers, mint with shard configuration,
manage profile + sovereign contracts, view rankings/stats.

## Brand personality / tone
"Quiet permanence" - archival, literate, materially serious, art-world credible. The opposite of
hype. A near-black digital conservatory: precise, calm, confident, restraint as the dominant tone.
NOT loud Web3, NOT neon crypto-bro. The art is always the brightest thing; the UI is the quiet frame.
Layout/IA should feel like OpenSea (dense, data-forward, ranked tables, filter rails, item pages),
rendered in this restrained, engineered aesthetic.

## Design tokens (authoritative)
- background #050505, surface #18181B, surface-2 #1f1f23
- border #27272A (hairline), border-bright #3f3f46
- text: foreground #FFFFFF, muted #A1A1AA, faint #71717A
- Signature accent: orchid pink #fe93ed (accent-dim #ee72de). Surgical use only (<5% of any screen):
  primary CTAs, active states, permanence-verified marks.
- verify-green #9EE6B4 for positive % change / live status; rose #fda4af for negative % change.
- Fonts: Inter (body/sans), JetBrains Mono (ALL verifiable values: hashes, addresses, ids, prices,
  timestamps, counts, eyebrow labels), Plus Jakarta Sans (font-brand: wordmark + large display headings).
- Radius: cards rounded-[10px], controls rounded-[8px], pills rounded-full. 8px spacing rhythm.
- Dark-mode-first and dark-mode-ONLY (no light mode in v1).

## Brand mark
"Fixed point": an unbroken white ring with a single pink (#fe93ed) center dot. Wordmark:
"perpetual" (Plus Jakarta Sans 600, -3.5% tracking) + pink "." + "art" (JetBrains Mono, muted).

## Hard guardrails
- Mono for every verifiable fact (the technical-trust fingerprint).
- Accent pink is a scalpel, not a paintbrush. Monochrome discipline is the premium signal.
- Motion: smooth, restrained, gallery not arcade. Ease-out / custom cubic-bezier, 200-600ms.
  No bounce/elastic. Respect prefers-reduced-motion.
- WebGL ambient field is a supporting background layer only, never competing with art.
- No long em/en dashes in copy (use commas/periods/hyphens).
- WCAG AA contrast, full keyboard nav, focus-visible states, semantic structure.
- The Permanence Status panel is the signature module; keep it prominent and independently verifiable.
